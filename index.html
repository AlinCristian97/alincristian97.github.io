About me

My journey with the Unity Engine started 2 years ago, when the company I was working for at the time offered its employees the chance to take part in a course of their choice. As someone who is passionate about videogames, I had always wanted to know how they are made, so Unity was my first option. Since then, I've been continuously learning how to program in C#, studied the concepts of Object-Oriented Programming and managed to get a solid grasp on operating Unity.

As for myself, I'm an optimist and a team player. I strive to inspire the same mindset in those around me. I like to keep my brain active, so most of my days are spent coding and studying. When it comes to relaxing I prefer to play videogames with my friends or watch a good TV show.

My goal is to work alongside a talented team and take part in the development of videogames.
